How long does the cabling and bracing process take, and will it require multiple visits?
Most cabling and bracing jobs can be completed in a single day, usually within a few hours per tree. However, if you have multiple trees or particularly large or complex cases, it may take longer. Yes, Couch Tree Service is fully licensed and insured to provide cabling and bracing services in Mississippi. Our arborists are trained to current industry standards and use only approved hardware and techniques to ensure the health and safety of your trees.
What is your process for determining if my tree needs cabling or bracing?
We start with a thorough inspection of your tree by a certified arborist, looking for signs like split limbs, weak branch unions, or storm damage. If cabling or bracing is recommended, we'll explain why and go over the options, including the types of hardware we use and how it will be installed. We also discuss follow-up care to ensure the long-term health of your tree.
